Daily Meal Plan

  1. Breakfast

    • Main Dish : Pancakes
      • Maple Syrup
      • Fresh Berries
      • Whipped Cream
    • Side : Fruit Salad
    • Drink : Orange Juice

  2. Lunch

    • Main Course : Build Your Own Sandwich
      1. Start with 2 slices of SourDough Bread
      2. Add a layer of Turkey Slices
      3. Place Swiss Cheese on top.
      4. Finish with Lettuce and Tomato.
    • Side : Potato Chips
    • Bevarage : Iced Tea

  3. Dinner

    • Appetizer : Garlic Bread
    • Main Course : Sphagetti Bolognese
      1. First, plate the cooked sphagetti
      2. Ladle the bolognese sauce over the pasta.
      3. Finally, sprinkle with grated Parmesan Cheese.
    • Dessert : Tiramisu
